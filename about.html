about us

Our Story

Committed to providing quality and value, one product at a time.

Founded in 2000 in Eket, Akwa Ibom, by Dr. Iniobong Etuk and Mrs. Uduak Etuk, Maniecu Stationery began as a small 250-square-foot shop selling basic stationery and past questions. With a mission to meet society's needs, we’ve grown into Eket’s most trusted stationery supplier.

Today, we continue to embrace innovation while staying true to our roots, offering top-quality products and personalized services to our customers.

From humble beginnings to becoming a cornerstone of our community, Maniecu Stationery remains dedicated to empowering individuals and businesses with the tools they need to succeed.

Our Vision

At Maniecu Stationery, our vision is to provide products that stand the test of time. We are dedicated to innovation, ensuring that our stationery not only meets today’s demands but also evolves with the future. From timeless tools to modern designs, we aim to inspire creativity and productivity for generations to come.
